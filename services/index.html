Competitions

UEFA organises some of the most prestigious and popular competitions in world football, uniting fans, players, and clubs across Europe.

Key Competitions Include:

  • UEFA Champions League
  • UEFA Europa League
  • UEFA Conference League
  • UEFA European Championship (EURO)
  • UEFA Nations League
  • UEFA Women's Champions League
  • UEFA Women's EURO

We manage all aspects from qualification draws and match scheduling to finals organisation and trophy presentations.
